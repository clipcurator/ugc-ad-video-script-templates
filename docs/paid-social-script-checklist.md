# Paid Social Script Checklist

## First 3 seconds

- The viewer knows who the ad is for.
- The pain or payoff appears immediately.
- The opening line sounds like a human, not a brochure.

## Body

- The script stays on one angle.
- The proof appears before the CTA.
- The lines are short enough for captions and voiceover.
- The ad can work even if watched without sound.

## CTA

- The CTA tells the viewer what to do next.
- The CTA matches the offer on the landing page.
- The CTA does not feel disconnected from the story.
